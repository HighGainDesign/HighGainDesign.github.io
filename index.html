About High Gain Design

High Gain Design provides strategic advisory services at the intersection of semiconductors, artificial intelligence, and business strategy. Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, we work with technology companies, startups, equity research analysts, and institutional investors seeking expert insights into the rapidly evolving semiconductor landscape.

With over 30 years of hands-on experience at industry leaders including Apple, Google, Tesla, AMD, and major gaming platforms, we offer a unique perspective on market dynamics, competitive positioning, and emerging technologies that shape investment decisions and business strategies.
